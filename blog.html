About Speakers

Deepa Chandrasekaran

Speaker 1

Author

Deepa Chandrasekharan is a Career Acceleration Expert, Leadership Facilitator, Keynote Speaker, Podcaster, Author, and Coach with 26 years of industry experience. Currently, she serves as the Convener for the CII IWN Telangana Mentorship Vertical for 2024-25. She firmly believes that each of us has the potential to create the life of our dreams, yet many are conditioned to believe in limits and conform to societal expectations. Through her work, she helps leaders develop faith in their own abilities to build lives that align with their true selves and aspirations. She is passionate about sharing her learnings and experiences with a wider audience.

Shrikant Sinha

Speaker 2

CEO, Telangana Academy for Skill and Knowledge

Shrikant Sinha, CEO of Telangana Academy for Skill and Knowledge (TASK), has been instrumental in transforming the state's skill development landscape. Under his leadership, TASK has trained over 500,000 students, significantly enhancing their employability and aligning their skills with industry demands. Sinha pioneered partnerships with leading companies and educational institutions, introducing cutting-edge programs in emerging technologies like AI, IoT, and cybersecurity. His efforts have led to a substantial increase in job placements and internships for students across Telangana. Sinha's achievements reflect his commitment to empowering youth and fostering a skilled workforce ready for the future.

Dr. Guru Prasad

Speaker 3

Managing Director, Kanaada Phonetics Pvt. Ltd

Guru Prasad is the inventor of the Kanaada Keyboard, a groundbreaking innovation designed to make typing in Indian languages easier and more efficient. Recognizing the challenges faced by users when typing in Kannada and other Indian scripts, Prasad developed the Kanaada Keyboard with a unique layout that simplifies the input process. His invention has been particularly impactful in bridging the digital divide, allowing more people to communicate and create content in their native languages. Prasad's work has garnered attention for its contribution to language preservation and technological accessibility, making him a notable figure in the field of digital innovation.

Yeshwanth Nag M

Speaker 4

Founder, The ThickShake Factory

Mr. M. Yeshwanth Nag (age 35 years) is the Founder of The ThickShake Factory – India’s 1st & largest Premium ThickShake Brand along with his co-founder Ashwin Mocherla(33 years). The ThickShake Factory has been a pioneer in the concept of speciality retailing of Shakes. Incepted in December 2013 in Hyderabad with a single store bootstrapped with a personal loan, TSF has steadily grown to 100+ stores by 2024 It has been recognized as one of the fastest growing QSR chains in India.

Dr. Vishwanand Pattar

Speaker 6

HR Business Partner

Chinna Gangaram Nagula

Speaker 7

Farmer

Mr. Nagula Chinna Gangaram is a farmer from the village of Nagulapalli in Telangana, India, known for his innovative farming techniques and commitment to sustainable agriculture. He gained recognition for adopting organic farming practices and using traditional methods to grow crops without relying on chemical fertilizers or pesticides. Gangaram's approach to farming emphasizes the use of natural resources and indigenous knowledge, which has helped him achieve high yields and improve soil health. His success story has inspired other farmers in the region to shift towards organic farming, contributing to a larger movement towards sustainable agriculture in Telangana. Gangaram's work is also seen as a model for preserving biodiversity and promoting environmental conservation in farming practices. He received several awards and recognitions for his contributions.

Dr. Sujatha R

Speaker 8

Principal - Entrepreneur Education, Wadhwani Entrepreneur

Dr. R. Sujatha is currently the Principal for Entrepreneur Education with Wadhwani Entrepreneur (WE), Wadhwani Foundation. She head the WE Academy faculties, consultants and conduct Faculty Orientation Programs to enable them deliver entrepreneurship courses. She also manage content for WE Academy programs offered for students aspiring to become entrepreneurs and Early stage start-ups. Earlier Dr. R. Sujatha served as a Professor with Amity Business School in the area of Organizational Behaviour, Human Resource and Entrepreneurship with rich teaching and research experience. She have 40 National and International conference papers, 50+ published research papers and cases in both National and International Journals. Also one of the authors for published books titled "Human Resource Management and for Organizational Change" and "Human Resource Management and the Implementation of Change" with Routledge Publication.
